What we are trying to learn

How operating judgement enters a capital decision.

Financial, legal and technical reviews do not always reconcile the investment case with recent operating performance, maintenance demands, contracts, staffing and regulatory conditions.

The study asks what is handled internally, when outside judgement is useful, who appoints that person and what the work must prove before a committee or board will rely on it.

It is market research. Participants are not asked to disclose confidential transaction, customer, employee or asset information.
